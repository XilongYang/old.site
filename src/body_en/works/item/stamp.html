Simple Two-Way Active Measure Protocol(IETF RFC8762).

Keyword: C++, STAMP, POSIX, Network

Simple Two-way Active Measurement Protocol (STAMP), which enables the measurement of both one-way and round-trip performance metrics, like delay, delay variation, and packet loss.
